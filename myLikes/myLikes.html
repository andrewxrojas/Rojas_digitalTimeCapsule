Sade


Sade holds a special place as one of my all-time favorite artists. Her voice has a unique ability to soothe my soul and transport me to a serene state. Whenever I'm laboring in my garden, immersed in the elements of nature, I invariably turn to Sade's music, which has a remarkable ability to make the strenuous work and discomfort simply fade away. Her music truly stands the test of time!



Physical


Rose Byrne has held a special place among my favorite actresses ever since her remarkable performance in the series "Damages" alongside Glenn Close. She possesses an incredible ability to transform herself, effortlessly taking on diverse roles. Her recent portrayal in the AppleTV+ series "Physical" resonates with me because it reminds me of people I know, albeit without the health struggles depicted in the show. Nevertheless, she excels in portraying this character and delving into the inner demons she must confront on her path to personal growth.



American Horror Story


American Horror Story holds a special place in my heart as one of my all-time favorite series. For a long time, a close friend and I had a weekly tradition: we would gather, prepare a delicious dinner, uncork a bottle of wine, and eagerly tune in to watch the latest episode. Every time I watch the show, a wave of nostalgia washes over me, bringing immense happiness. It's a delightful escape that effortlessly sweeps away any stress I might be dealing with, allowing me to fully immerse myself in the world of the show, if only for that fleeting moment.
