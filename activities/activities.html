San Gregorio Beach is one of my hidden treasures! I adore visiting this beach whenever the weather is beautiful. It's a wonderfully spacious private beach, completely secluded from the public. Even on chilly days, you'll still find me here because it's simply perfect!

Marshall Beach holds a special place in my heart because it was one of the first beaches I visited when I first moved to Northern California. It's a bit of an adventure to reach, but once you arrive, you are greeted with a lovely private beach and a breathtaking view of the Golden Gate Bridge.

Secret Cove is another one of my favorites! Whenever I get the chance to visit Tahoe, you can bet you'll find me here. This stunning lake boasts crystal-clear blue waters, framed by massive boulders that create a natural barrier from the public area. These tranquil waters provide a refreshing experience and an excellent opportunity for kayaking.
